About Me

Who am I?

I'm an entrepreneur, researcher, educator, and innovation organizer based in Vienna, Austria — with over a decade of experience spanning information security research, sustainability technology, and financial education. I studied at WU Wien (Wirtschaftsuniversität Wien) and built my career across 25+ roles internationally, including stops in Reykjavik and London. Today I lead Sustainista (ESG automation with AI), co-run Skills-UP! (financial literacy for apprentices — TOP 3 MEGA Education Million 2025), teach at TU Wien and FH WKW, and organize the Europe Tech Hackathon at ViennaUP.

Featured Project · Education

Skills-UP! — Financial Literacy for Apprentices

Skills-UP! brings financial education where it's needed most: directly into the daily life of Austrian vocational schools. Instead of textbooks, real apprentices guide their peers through savings goals, debt traps, crypto basics, and emergency budgets — using gamification, quiz battles, podcasts, and peer-made short videos.
