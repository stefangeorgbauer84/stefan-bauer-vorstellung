Featured Project · Financial Education

Skills-UP! — Financial Literacy for Apprentices

Skills-UP! brings financial education where it's needed most: directly into Austrian vocational schools. Instead of textbooks, real apprentices guide their peers through savings goals, debt traps, crypto basics, and emergency budgets — using gamification, quiz battles, podcasts, and peer-made videos. 90% of participants report lasting behavioral change.

ERASMUS+ Project · EU Funded

L2BGreen — Green Entrepreneurship for Europe

L2BGreen is an Erasmus+ initiative I co-lead — equipping people across Europe with the skills for green entrepreneurship. The project builds an interactive online course, an AI chatbot for real-world case studies, and a Europe-wide community network. I serve as Work Package Lead and co-founder of the technology partner building the AI chatbot component.
