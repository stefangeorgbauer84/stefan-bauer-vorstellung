ERASMUS+ Project · EU Funded

L2BGreen — Green Entrepreneurship for Europe

L2BGreen is an Erasmus+ initiative I co-lead as part of the consortium — equipping people across Europe with the skills and knowledge for green entrepreneurship. The project creates an interactive online course, an AI chatbot for real-world case studies, and a Europe-wide community. I serve as Work Package Lead and co-founder of the technology partner building the AI chatbot component.

FFG Flagship Project · Digital Product Passport

PACE-DPP & DPP Austria — Circular Economy

The Digital Product Passport (DPP) is becoming mandatory under EU law — for batteries, textiles, electronics, and more. As Work Package Lead in the FFG flagship project PACE-DPP and partner of dpp-austria.at, Sustainista is building the tools and data ecosystems that make DPP implementation practical for Austrian and European companies. Funded by the Austrian Federal Ministry for Climate Action (BMK) and the FFG.

EU Battery Regulation Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation Data Spaces & Digital Twins Circular Economy Business Models Austrian FFG Flagship funded Coordinated by Virtual Vehicle Research GmbH
