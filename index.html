Featured Project

Skills-UP! — Financial Literacy for Apprentices

Skills-UP! is Austria's leading peer-to-peer financial education platform designed for apprentices aged 14–20. Instead of textbooks, real apprentices guide their peers through topics like savings goals, debt traps, crypto basics, and emergency budgeting. The platform uses gamification, quiz battles, podcasts, and short videos to create lasting behavioral change.
